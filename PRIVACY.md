# Privacy Policy — Wingman

_Last updated: June 2026_

Wingman is a browser extension that reads the text of the chat conversation visible in your active tab and suggests replies based on a goal you select. This policy explains what the extension accesses, how that information is used, and what is (and isn't) stored.

## What Wingman accesses

- **Visible conversation text.** When you click "Suggest replies," Wingman reads the text of the conversation currently shown on the page so it can generate relevant reply options. It only reads what is already visible on your screen.
- **Your inputs.** The goal/vibe you select and any optional text you type into the panel.
- **Your API key.** You provide your own Groq API key, which is stored locally in your browser so you don't have to re-enter it.

## How that information is used

- The visible conversation text, your selected goal, and your typed input are sent to the Groq API (Llama model) for the sole purpose of generating reply suggestions, which are shown back to you in the extension.
- Your API key is used only to authenticate those requests to Groq.

## What Wingman stores

- **Locally, in your browser only:** your API key, your theme preference, and the saved position of the floating button. This data never leaves your device except as part of the API request described above.
- **Nothing is stored on any server operated by the developer.** Wingman has no backend, no database, and no analytics. The developer does not collect, log, see, or retain your conversations.

## What Wingman does NOT do

- It does not sell or share your data with third parties.
- It does not store your conversations anywhere.
- It does not send messages on your behalf — you choose whether to insert or copy any suggestion.
- It does not track your browsing activity.

## Third-party service

Reply suggestions are generated by the Groq API. When you request suggestions, the relevant conversation text is transmitted to Groq for processing. Please review Groq's own privacy policy at https://groq.com/privacy-policy/ for how they handle data sent to their API.

## Your control

- You can remove your stored API key and preferences at any time by removing the extension or clearing its storage.
- Wingman only activates when you open it and click to generate suggestions.
